February 22, 2015

HEC Paris in Qatar Hosts French Minister for Foreign Trade

Share
HEC Paris School of Management recently hosted a conference by Matthias Fekl, the French Minister for Foreign Trade, the Promotion of Tourism and French Nationals Abroad, during his official visit to Qatar.

At the conference, attended by high ranking Qatari officials, top executives of public and private firms, and alumni of HEC Paris, Mr Fekl reviewed the various aspects of the economic relationship between France and Qatar. He also gave an appraisal of the current economic situation in France and the ongoing reforms aimed at reducing unemployment and increasing competitiveness.

He pointed out that foreign companies in the French economy represent 30 percent of total industrial investment, 30 percent of Research & Development and one third of French exports.

Accompanied by 30 French companies, the objectives of his visit were to promote bilateral economic cooperation between Qatar and France as well as to foster stronger ties between the French and Qatari private sector.
